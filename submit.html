Submitting a Run

In order to submit a run:

  1. Ensure you are following the rules for your Category. Currently we follow the same rules as found on the SRC page, except we allow the use of the turbo button.
  2. Upload or higlight your VOD permanently (If your VOD link becomes unavailable, it will be removed form the leaderboard.
  3. Email us at submissions@turbodread.com with your submission, and ensure it contains the following:
    1. Your username you would like displayed on the leaderboard
    2. Your Country-If you'd prefer to leave this out, you can :)
    3. The category you are submitting for
    4. Your time (both RTA and IGT)
    5. The link to your VOD
    6. The version of the game you are running (You can find this by pressing + on the game from the Switch menu.)
    7. OPTIONAL: If you would like a shoutout on Twitter, please say "I would like a shoutout on Twitter." and include your twitter handle if you'd also like a mention.
    8. OPTIONAL: If you would like, you can also include why accessibility options like the turbo button are important in speedrunning for you, and I may include it on the About page. Please also include if you would like your name attached with the quote, or it will be posted anonymously.
